Tax Optimization Strategies in India for FIRE

1. Section 80C:
- PPF
- ELSS mutual funds
- Life insurance

2. Section 80CCD(1B):
- National Pension System (NPS)

3. Capital Gains:
- Prefer long-term investments
- Lower tax rates compared to short-term

4. Avoid:
- Frequent trading (high tax + fees)

Goal:
Minimize taxes to maximize wealth accumulation.
