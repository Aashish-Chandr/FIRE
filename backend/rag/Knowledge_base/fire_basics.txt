FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) - India Guide

FIRE means achieving financial independence so that you can retire early and live off your investments.

FIRE Corpus Formula:
FIRE Corpus = Annual Expenses × 25 to 35

Example:
If annual expenses are ₹8,00,000
Target corpus = ₹2 crore to ₹2.8 crore

In India, this must consider:
- Inflation (especially healthcare)
- Lack of strong social security
- Family responsibilities

Safe Withdrawal Rate:
- 3% to 3.5% annually is considered safe

Goal:
Build enough wealth so that your investments generate passive income for life.
