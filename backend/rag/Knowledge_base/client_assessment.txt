Client Financial Assessment for FIRE Planning

Before creating a FIRE plan, evaluate:

1. Income:
- Sources of income
- Stability and growth potential

2. Expenses:
- Monthly spending habits
- Lifestyle inflation trends

3. Dependents:
- Family members relying on income
- Future obligations (education, marriage)

4. Assets:
- Savings, investments, property

5. Risk Appetite:
- Ability to handle market crashes
- Emotional behavior during losses

6. Retirement Vision:
- Desired lifestyle after retirement
- Travel, luxury, or minimal living

This assessment helps create a personalized FIRE strategy.
