Insurance Planning for FIRE in India

1. Term Insurance:
- Coverage: 15–20× annual income
- Purpose: Protect family financially

2. Health Insurance:
- Minimum ₹10–20 lakh coverage
- Essential due to rising healthcare costs

3. Family Floater Plan:
- Covers entire family under one policy

Importance:
- Prevents financial shocks
- Protects FIRE corpus from unexpected expenses
